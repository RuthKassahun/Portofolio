ETHIO-SCHOLARS

Ethio-Scholars is a digitalized online research papers repository platform. It is a place where outstanding research papers all over the country are centrally stored. Universities and Independent researchers (non-academic researchers) can upload their research papers. On the other hand, enterprises and users can download any research paper they are looking for. An uploader (it could be univesity researcher or an independent researcher) will be paid for each research paper that is downloaded. Additionally, we offer grant opportunities for academic and non-academic researchers to finance their research. The platform will disseminate quality research papers for the right user at the right time. Every individual and organizations can make connection with the researchers to work together.
